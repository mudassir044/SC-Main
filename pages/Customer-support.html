Frequently Asked Questions

What is Customer Support Outsourcing?

Customer Support Outsourcing is the process of hiring a third-party provider to handle customer service functions such as live chat, phone support, email support, and more. It helps businesses reduce costs while maintaining high-quality customer service.

Why should I outsource my customer support?

Outsourcing customer support allows you to cut operational costs, access a trained workforce, provide 24/7 service, scale easily, and focus on core business operations while ensuring customer satisfaction.

What types of customer support services do you offer?

We offer live chat support, phone support, email support, technical support (L1, L2, L3), social media support, multilingual support, and back-office support including order processing, billing, and data management.

Where are your customer support agents located?

Our primary operations are based in Pakistan, providing cost-effective, high-quality offshore support. We also offer onshore and nearshore options upon request.

What are your pricing plans?

Our pricing starts at $8/hour for basic customer support services. We offer flexible plans based on the level of support you need, including Pro and Premium plans with additional services.

Can I scale up or down as needed?

Yes! Our services are fully scalable. You can increase or decrease the number of agents or services as your business needs change.

Do you offer multilingual support?

Yes! We offer multilingual support in English, Urdu, Arabic, Spanish, and more. Let us know your requirements, and we’ll provide the right language support.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy! Contact us via phone, email, or live chat, and we’ll help you choose the best plan based on your business needs.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 3-day risk-free trial so you can experience our services before committing.
